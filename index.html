Who I Am

Crafting Brands With Purpose & Precision

I'm Oyinloye-Olabisi Oreoluwa, the creative mind behind Blazing — a personal brand built on the belief that every brand deserves a visual identity as powerful as its story. Over the past 4 years, I've transformed ideas into striking visual experiences.

I specialize in brand identity, logo design, and digital visuals that communicate with clarity and impact. My process blends strategic thinking with creative execution, ensuring every design decision serves a purpose and resonates with your audience.

Whether you're launching a new brand or revamping an existing one, I bring the same attention to detail, passion for storytelling, and commitment to excellence to every project.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you work with international clients?

Yes — I collaborate with clients worldwide through digital meetings, clear timelines, and smooth file delivery, no matter where you're based.

What do you need from me to get started?

Share your brand goals, target audience, favorite styles, examples you like, and any existing assets or color preferences.

How long does a branding project usually take?

Most branding projects run 2–4 weeks depending on scope, with room for revisions and feedback to make sure the result matches your vision.

What's included in your design packages?
Brand Identity
Complete visual system for your brand
  • Primary logo system: colour, stacked, mono, icon
  • Logo variations for print, web, and favicon use
  • Brand colour palette with HEX, RGB & CMYK codes
  • Custom typography pairing for consistent styling
  • Brand guidelines with usage rules and examples
Social Media Flyer
Scroll-stopping content for every platform
  • Post graphics sized for Instagram, Facebook & LinkedIn
  • Brand-consistent styling across all visuals
  • Ready-to-use social media assets
  • JPEG & PNG exports delivered by email
Flyer Design
Eye-catching print & digital flyers
  • Print-ready files in CMYK, 300dpi with bleed
  • Digital version optimised for web and messaging apps
  • Up to 3 revision rounds included
  • JPEG, PNG & PDF files delivered by email
What's your pricing structure?

My pricing depends on the type of service and the scope of your project. A brand identity package, for example, is priced differently from a single flyer or a set of social media graphics, because each comes with its own level of detail, deliverables, and time investment.

For a full breakdown of my rates, you can view my rate card here View Rate Card

If your project has specific requirements that don't fit neatly into a standard package, feel free to reach out directly and I'll put together a custom quote for you.

Design Process

How I Bring Brands to Life

Every project moves through a clear, collaborative process so your brand arrives polished, purposeful, and ready to make an impact.

01

Collaborate

We start with discovery: your business goals, audience, and vision to ensure alignment from day one.

02

Research

I analyze market trends, competitors, and audience insights to uncover positioning that sets your brand apart.

03

Apply

Concepts come to life through mood boards, logo exploration, typography, palettes, and visual identity development.

04

Fix

Your feedback is carefully applied to refine every detail and strengthen the overall brand system.

05

Transfer

Final brand assets are delivered in professional formats, fully organized and ready for real-world launch.
